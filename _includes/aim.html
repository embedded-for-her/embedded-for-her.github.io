Aim

{{site.title}} is a skill development program in embedded systems for women.

Understand fundamentals

Does a LED always glow when you drive a signal high? What's stored in Flash and RAM? Strengthen your fundamentals to take these in your stride.

Develop skill set

Learn skills relevant for an embedded systems developer. Including peripheral interfacing, compiling & linking, debugging, circuit development, sensor/actuator interfacing and many more.

Enhance your portfolio

Add another project to your portfolio, this time a complete embedded project. Let it shine in your CV and interviews. Make a video of it working, explain it in a blog and let it reach out to the community.

Get inspired

The ability to make things made of plastic and silicon do your bidding can give quite a rush. Get to know and share the satisfaction of making an idea in your head work in the physical world.

Build confidence

Be it your next freelancing task or a project for a competition, you know you are up for the challenge. You can prove your worth when companies are looking for competent embedded engineers.

Connect to start-up ecosystem

On the final session get the chance to connect and impress the start-up community in Bangalore in the embedded systems space.
